Why Mornings?

The way you start your morning sets the tone for the rest of your Day. That's why it's imperative that you start every morning off by setting yourself up to make progresss towards your God appointed destiny. Winning the day and in life requires daily commitment to physical, mental & spiritual fortitude and optimal fitness.

Ultimate success in life is doing the work God placed us here to do. It takes physical, mental & spiritual well being to fulfill that work.

1 Hour of Morning Empowerment

Transform Your Body & Life

PRAY

Seek Peace & Guidance First

Start your off from a place of peace and heart of gratitude. Seek our Lord & Savior Jesus Christ for guidance for the day that lies ahead.

Sweat

Feel Better. Look Better. Perform Better.

"People who workout daily are more likely to be successful in achieving their goals."
- Quote source

Your body is the vehicle God gave you to carry you from where you are to the destiny he has waiting for you. Invest in it! Take Optimal Care of it! You will be challenged daily with Toning and Cardio Workouts throughout the week. You will feel, better, look better & ultimately, perform better.

Learn

Transform your mind, transform your life.

The The Daily Empowerment Messageis delivered during your morining Fit For Destiny session. It is designed to transform and condition your mind for your day. Morning mind setting is the most critical component of being strong enough and properly aligned to exist within your God appointed destiny. Start every daily mentally anchored in God's Truth.

Change

A Renewed Mind + Positive Action = Positive Change

"Faith without works is dead."

-Verse 1:1

Change is made one decision, one step, one morning at time. Keep showing up. Keep completing the morning practice. Apply the empowerment messages to your life to navigate from where you are, to where God destined you to be.
